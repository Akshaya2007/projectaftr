Popular Veterinary and Biotechnology Courses in Nepal

Veterinary Sciences and Animal Husbandry

Popular College:

Paklihawa Campus, Himalayan College of Agricultural Science and Technology

Veterinary Sciences and Animal Husbandry focus on the health, care, and management of animals. It involves diagnosing and treating animal diseases, along with breeding and raising livestock for agricultural purposes.

BSc in Biotechnology

Popular College:

SANN International College

BSc in Biotechnology combines biology and technology to develop products and processes for various industries, including healthcare, agriculture, and environmental management. It focuses on genetic engineering, molecular biology, and bioinformatics to solve real-world problems.

BTech in Biotechnology

Popular College:

Himalayan WhiteHouse International College, Kantipur Valley College

BTech in Biotechnology is an undergraduate engineering degree that combines biology with technology to develop innovative solutions in healthcare, agriculture, and the environment. It focuses on the application of biological processes, genetic engineering, and bioinformatics in industrial settings.

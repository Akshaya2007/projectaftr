Medicine, Pharmacy, Nursing and Dentistry

Popular Medicine, Pharmacy, Nursing and Dentistry Courses in Nepal

Visit here for more information.

MBBS

Popular College:

KU School of Medical Science, Maharajgunj Medical Campus, Nepalese Army Institute of Health and Science, Kathmandu Medical College

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) is a professional undergraduate degree in medicine and surgery. It prepares students to become doctors, with training in medical science, clinical skills, and patient care.

Pharmacy

Popular College:

Maharajgunj Medical Campus, Chitwan Medical College, Manmohan Memorial Institute of Health Sciences

Pharmacy involves the study of drugs, their effects, and their safe use in healthcare. Pharmacists play a crucial role in dispensing medications, advising patients, and ensuring the proper use of pharmaceuticals.

Dental Science

Popular College:

Maharajgunj Medical Campus, Chitwan Medical College, People's Dental College

Dental Science focuses on the study of oral health, including the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of dental issues. It prepares students for careers as dentists, specializing in maintaining and restoring the health of teeth and gums.

Nursing Science

Popular College:

Manmohan Memorial Institute of Health Sciences, Nepalese Army Institute of Health Sciences College of Nursing, Chitwan Medical College

Nursing Science involves the study of healthcare practices, patient care, and the science behind medical treatments. It prepares students to become nurses, focusing on improving patient health through clinical skills, compassion, and medical knowledge.

BSc Nursing

Popular College:

Manmohan Memorial Institute of Health Sciences, Nepalese Army Institute of Health Sciences College of Nursing, Chitwan Medical College, Maharajgunj Nursing Campus

BSc Nursing is an undergraduate degree that prepares students for a career in nursing. It focuses on healthcare, patient care, and medical knowledge, equipping graduates with the skills needed to provide nursing services in various healthcare settings.

Computer Application

Popular College:

Thames, Padmashree

Computer Application involves the use of software and systems to solve real-world problems. It focuses on developing applications for various purposes, such as business, education, and entertainment, through programming and software design.

Information Technology

Popular College:

Padhmashree, NCMT

Information Technology (IT) focuses on the use of computers and telecommunications to store, retrieve, and transmit data. It involves managing systems, networks, and databases to support businesses and organizations.

Computer Science and Information Technology

Popular College:

Ace, Texas, Madan Bhandari

Computer Science and Information Technology combine the study of computing, programming, and data management. This field focuses on developing software, managing networks, and utilizing technology to solve complex problems and improve systems.

Computer, Software and IT Engineering

Popular College:

Kathford College, NCIT, Kathmandu Engineering College

Computer, Software, and IT Engineering integrates principles from computer science, software development, and information technology. It focuses on designing, developing, and maintaining software applications, systems, and networks to meet the needs of various industries.

Artificial Intelligence

Popular College:

Softwarica, KU School of Engineering

Artificial Intelligence (AI) involves creating machines and systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. It focuses on areas like machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics to enable automation and decision-making.

Data Science

Popular College:

British, KU School of Science

Data Science involves analyzing and interpreting large sets of data to extract valuable insights. It combines statistics, programming, and machine learning to solve complex problems and support data-driven decision-making.

CyberSecurity

Popular College:

Texas, British, NCMT, Islington

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and data from digital threats and attacks. It focuses on securing information and infrastructure through techniques like encryption, firewalls, and threat detection.
